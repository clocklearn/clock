English Recording Scripts
========================

1. lesson1_intro.mp3
--------------------
Hello! In this lesson we will learn about the basic clock components. We will learn about numbers, hands, and the center.

2. numbers.mp3
--------------
The clock has twelve numbers from one to twelve. These numbers help us tell the time.

3. hour_hand.mp3
----------------
The hour hand is the short hand. It points to the current hour and moves slowly.

4. minute_hand.mp3
------------------
The minute hand is the long hand. It points to the minutes and moves faster than the hour hand.

5. center.mp3
-------------
The center is the point where all the hands meet. It's like the heart of the clock.

Notes:
- Speak clearly and slowly
- Use a friendly and encouraging tone
- Make sure there's no background noise
- Each file should be between 5-15 seconds
